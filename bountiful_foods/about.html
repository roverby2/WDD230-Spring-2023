Company Info

Welcome to Bountiful Foods, your premier destination for organic, locally sourced foods that nourish the body and soul. Our journey began and continues in the heart of Carlsbad, California, where our small-scale enterprise has been steadfastly growing and nurturing a dedicated community of health-conscious consumers. We are proud, not just of the business we've built, but of the role we play in fostering healthier, more sustainable lifestyles with every product we offer. At Bountiful Foods, our ethos is to offer customers not just food, but a medley of fresh and wholesome choices that are as delicious as they are nutritious. We handpick an assortment of produce directly from the gardens and orchards of local farmers. These are individuals who, like us, have a passion for their craft and an unwavering commitment to sustainable farming practices. This ensures you not only get the freshest foods, but also those cultivated without compromising the environment. Beyond our local community, we've also established partnerships with national and international suppliers. These relationships are built upon a shared ethos of quality and sustainability. We believe in the importance of extending our organic, wholesome food ethos across borders, collaborating with like-minded suppliers who are equally driven by standards of quality and sustainable practices. This commitment to sourcing locally and organically is more than just our business model; it's our way of promoting healthier communities, one plate at a time. So, welcome to Bountiful Foods, where we serve not just meals, but a commitment to a healthier, more sustainable world.

Bountiful Foods History

Welcome to Bountiful Foods, your ultimate source for locally grown, organic foods. Rooted in Carlsbad, California, our small but dedicated business is proud to offer our customers wholesome, fresh food choices. We procure our produce directly from nearby farmers, in addition to partnering with national and international suppliers who are equally committed to quality and sustainable practices.

Locations

Our Carlsbad retail outlet welcomes walk-ins, providing an expansive assortment of fruits and produce. Furthermore, our mobile food trucks traverse the coastal stretch from Los Angeles to San Diego, ensuring that healthy and tasty food options are accessible in your local area. To maximize convenience, we gladly take orders via phone calls and through our online platform.

Mission

At Bountiful Foods, our mission is to promote healthy eating habits among the local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas. With a focus on organic bounty and convenience, we offer made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags that are perfect for a quick, nutritious snack or meal on the go.

We invite you to drop by our Carlsbad store or locate one of our food trucks to experience the unique flavor and quality of organic, locally sourced foods first-hand. We eagerly await the opportunity to serve you.!
