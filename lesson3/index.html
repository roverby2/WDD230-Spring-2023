Site Name:

Seattle Metropolitan Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Seattle Metropolitan Chamber of Commerce website is to provide local businesses with information and resources so that they can prosper and grow. The site includes historical information, current graphics, a directory of local businesses, a way to become a member, and contact information. It will also serve as a resource for residents and visitors to learn about the vibrant business community in Seattle.
